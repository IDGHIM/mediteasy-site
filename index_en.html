Mediteasy

A mobile meditation app to support your daily practice. Open source and free!

About the Project

Mediteasy is a meditation mobile app built with Ionic. It offers customizable timers, soothing background sounds, and progress tracking to help you maintain a regular meditation routine. Compatible with iOS and Android, the app is currently under active development.
